For Irina

I have seen through your soul.
I don't know your face, I don't know your age.
But if there is in the world a place
Is in Krasnodar
If the world has placed us so far apart,
there must be a reason:
Perhaps i am meant to find you among the flowers you tend so well.
Argentina / 18 de Abril de 2026
